When?

Classes build up week-on-week, with new dances added or extra steps learnt in the dances the children already know.

Improvers carries on from the Summer term 2:30-3:30pm slot.

Beginners class is suitable for everyone and runs 3:30pm-4:30pm.

If you're unsure which class to attend, talk to us at a lesson or contact us online for advice.
