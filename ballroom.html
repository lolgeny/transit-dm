Ballroom Dances

The Ballroom (or standard) dances are romantic, jazzy or passionate. In competitions the man wears a white tie and tailsuit and the lady usually wears a long, floaty dress to best show off the spins around the floor. All the dances are "in hold" which means the lady stands close to the man and the couple dance as a single unit.
