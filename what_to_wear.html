Shoes

Wear light and comfortable shoes for your first few lessons whilst you are getting used to the steps. You could wear Jazz shoes if you have them already. Try not to come wearing trainers, as they have too much grip. As you start to understand the basic steps of the dances, you may wish to purchase specialised Ballroom/Latin shoes. These have a suede sole to help control your grip around the floor. They have less grip than Jazz shoes so they will allow you to slide better!

Dance Magic sell some basic Ballroom and Latin shoes to get you started. No need to pay now - send us the style and size that you wish to purchase and we will have your new pair of shoes to try on at the class in usually under a week.
