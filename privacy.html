Privacy Policy

We care about your privacy and want you to feel safe on our site.

Why your data is collected

If your child requires parental help during a class, we will need to contact you. If you opt in to receive e-mails, we can also inform you of upcoming classes and other news of interest. We do not share your personal data with anyone else.

How your data is collected

We ask you to fill out a registration form when joining the class for the first time. Anonymous website data is obtained for Google Analytics when you browse our website.

Where your data is processed

Google Analytics processes anonymous website data as a third party. Otherwise all data processing is done by us alone.

You're in control

If you want to know what data we hold on you, change your preference for receiving e-mails or remove yourself entirely from our systems, just let us know either at class or by e-mailing info@dance-magic.co.uk.
